📖 Reporting Protocol Manual

Step 1: Entity Identification

Identify the "Support Company" or "Finance Entity" involved. Document organization numbers, registered addresses in Molde, Ålesund, or Kristiansund, and suspected insider contacts.

Step 2: SWIFT & Wire Trace

Aggregate all transaction logs. For FBI (IC3) reports, include IP addresses used during the swindle. For Europol, flag any cross-border VAT or finance fraud triggers.

Step 3: Agency Submission

  • FBI (IC3): Used for US-linked wire fraud and cyber swindles.
  • INTERPOL (I-24/7): For transnational serial fraud and parole violators.
  • EUROPOL (EC3): Specialized in European finance crimes and asset steering.

Step 4: Asset Recovery Protocol

Engage the "Condition Settle" protocol. Request a "Freeze Order" on all accounts linked to the swindle commission to prevent asset dissipation.
