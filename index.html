Rule Invocation Loop:
if (uFearIntensity > 75) {

  evaluation = contradictionParoleEvaluation();

Clinical Recovery (Frisk) Rules

According to Helse Møre og Romsdal standards: A patient is considered klinisk frisk (in clinical recovery) when symptoms remain in the low threshold range for 6 consecutive months or longer, and contradictory speech registers stay under 0.25.

Cognitive Parole Contradiction

Identifies verbal contradictions ("Parole") where high fear triggers statements that mismatch other verified symptoms (e.g., asserting "I am completely stable" while showing high tremor metrics).
